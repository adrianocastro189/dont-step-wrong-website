Don't Step Wrong 2 is a sequel built on the same core idea as the original
itch.io game: each level has one implicit rule that the player discovers
through trial and error. The sequel expands the formula in three directions.

## New mechanics

The first game had a small set of rule categories. DSW2 has dozens, organized
in systems that the player encounters progressively.

## Biomes

Each biome introduces a new visual identity and a new family of rules. The
Green Forest is where you start — it teaches the system. Later biomes layer
new constraints on top.

## Skills

Players now have access to skills that reveal hints without breaking the
core discovery loop. Spider Sense, Breadcrumb Trail, Dead End Sense — each
one helps in a specific moment without telling you the answer.
